IntellectoNova is a smart study planner with a stunning UI. It's designed to help students organize their subjects and topics, and then leverage AI to create an optimized study plan with actionable recommendations.

### âœ¨ Key Features

* **Modern Glassmorphic UI:** A clean and aesthetic "glass" effect on all cards, set against a soft purple gradient.
* **AI Assistant:** Chat directly with an AI (powered by OpenRouter/Mistral) for general study tips and help.
* **Smart Subject Input:** Add subjects with details like exam date, topic, difficulty, and importance.
* **Automated Schedule Generation:** Creates a baseline schedule by prioritizing subjects based on your inputs (difficulty, importance, days left).
* **ðŸ§  AI-Powered Recommendations:** The app sends your basic schedule to the AI to get *actionable, high-level advice* on study techniques and time allocation, which is displayed directly above your plan.
* **Dynamic & Responsive:** The subject list, chat, and schedule all update in real-time without a page refresh.
